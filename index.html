OUR VACANCIES

Welcome to Miracle Gardens Nursery!

Miracle Gardens is a privately owned, newly opened nursery, which offers a warm and friendly welcome to all the neighbouring families of our local community. Based on beautiful grounds of St Mary’s School, the nursery benefits from our existing extensive educational facilities and an enviable outdoor learning space. The building provides a wonderful learning environment with stimulating playroom offering direct outdoor access. The outdoor area has opportunities for a wide range of activities and experiences which provide challenging opportunities for exploration and the development of physical skills. The Miracle Gardens nursery is open plan, with accommodation for 30 children aged between one and five years old. Our dedicated team is passionate about providing your child with the best possible start in life. Through our play-based curriculum, we ignite children’s curiosity, encourage their natural creativity, and nurture their love for learning. We promote parent partnership and provide regular updates about your child’s progress. We are absolutely delighted with our wonderful new nursery which is a fantastic addition to Early Years settings in the centre of Chessington. We look forward to working with all the families in our local community. Please come and visit us!
